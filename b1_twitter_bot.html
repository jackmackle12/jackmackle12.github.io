Introduction

Since January 2021 I have held significant positions in the uranium sector thanks to the advice of a friend. For the uninitiated, there is growing demand for nuclear fuel and supply uncertainty due to years of under-investment. Look into it. Please note: this sector will rip your face off.

The uranium investment thesis is heavily discussed on Twitter. Or at least I find it is. I wanted to contribute to the community and showcase my data skills through a personal project, so I decided to create u3o8_botman. This bot is powered by a virtual machine (VM) and uses VADER - a sentiment analysis tool - to determine the daily average sentiment of tweets containing '#uranium'. Is this information useful? Probably not, but it was fun to make.

This blog will outline how I built the bot, specifically focusing on:

  • Using the Tweepy library
  • Using the VADER library
  • Provisioning a virtual machine
  • Automating scripts with crontab
